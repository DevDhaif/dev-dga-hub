# Code of conduct

Be respectful. This project welcomes contributors of every background, in
English and Arabic alike.

Not acceptable, anywhere in this project's spaces (issues, PRs, discussions):

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Publishing someone's private information
- Sustained disruption or bad-faith arguing

Maintainers may edit or remove content that breaks these rules and may block
repeat offenders.

To report a problem, email devdhaif@gmail.com. Reports are handled privately.
